Founder story

Inspired by Sydney and Cooper.

This company was inspired by my two labradoodles, Sydney and Cooper.

We love playing fetch. At the beach, the park, the lake — we could go for hours. It is their favorite thing in the world.

But every time we finished, I ran into the same problem: I had nowhere to put the dirty, slobbery tennis ball. I started using poop bags, but it felt wasteful and, honestly, it was still messy. On top of that, I did not always have a tennis ball on me when we wanted to play.

I wanted something simple: a clean way to carry the ball and always have it ready.

That is why I created Ball Capsule — to keep the mess contained and the game always within reach.

LA SYD is named in honor of Sydney, who inspired this whole idea.
